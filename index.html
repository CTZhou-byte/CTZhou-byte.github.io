    About Me

    • I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Gongshen Liu and Prof. Zhuosheng Zhang.
    • I received my M.S. degree in 2022 from Jiangsu University, supervised by Prof. Mu Han. I am also supervised by Prof. Fengwei Zhang in Southern University of Science and Technology. I received my B.S. degree in 2019 from Nanjing University of Science and Technology.
    • My general research interest lies in natural language processing, AI security (Backdoor attack, Watermark, Privacy), cybersecurity, and LLM (Agent, Reasoning).
    • I have published over 15 papers in top-tier conferences and journals, including TNNLS, ACL, AAAI, NAACL, FGCS, IJIS, P2P, and Vehicular Communication. Among my notable achievements, I was recognized as an Academic Star at Jiangsu University and received the National Scholarship and Cybersecurity Scholarships. Furthermore, I have been awarded in various competitions, such as Cryptography and Mathematical Modeling. I have actively contributed to the academic community by serving as a reviewer for prestigious conferences and journals such as TITS, TVT, NeurIPS, ICLR, ACL, AAAI, and others.
